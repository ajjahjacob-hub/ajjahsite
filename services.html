Residential Design Services

Small-office scope focused on clear, buildable documentation and practical outcomes. Work is quality-checked and, where required, coordinated with licensed professionals for compliance. I'm a trainee architectural designer (Diploma in progress); discounted rates apply while in training.

Scope of Services

  • Pre-design & advice: site visit, brief development, feasibility, budget guidance, planning risks.
  • Site & measure: as-built measurements, photos, basic site analysis (sun, wind, views, access).
  • Concept design: plan options, massing studies, room flow, faÃ§ade ideas, preliminary materials.
  • Developed design: refined plans/elevations/sections, key details, fixture and material selections.
  • Building consent drawings ( NZBC-aligned): full documentation for council lodgement, specifications, bracing and lintel schedules, E2/AS1 weathertight details (as required).
  • Resource consent support (if needed): district plan review, basic assessment drawings, liaison with planners.

Project Types

  • Additions & alterations: extensions, reconfigurations, sleepouts, garages, decks, kitchens, bathrooms.
  • New homes & minor dwellings: compact to mid-scale houses, transportable/tiny-home-friendly layouts.
  • Interiors: kitchen/bath design, joinery layouts, lighting plans, finishes palettes.

Sustainability & Performance

  • Insulation upgrades, airtightness basics, ventilation and daylight improvements, material guidance.

Coordination & Approvals

  • Consultant coordination: structural engineer, survey, geotech, energy, fire (as scope requires).
  • Council liaison: application preparation, submissions, responses to RFIs, and revisions.

Delivery & Build Support

  • Tender & build support: issuing drawings to builders, addenda/clarifications, shop-drawing review.
  • Digital deliverables: Archicad models, PDFs, schedules; learning Revit; optional 3D views and simple renders; VR-ready where practical.

Accessibility & Safety

  • Basic universal-design considerations and CPTED-aware planning.
